FAQ

What advice do you have for my lack of confidence in my own art?

It is hard to have confidence in your art. You find tiny errors and issues in your own art so it is indeed hard to do.

I also have a playlist you might find helpful: this playlist is to help you better yourself from doubt.

All I can say is I also go through a vulnerable stage whenever I finish something (or sometimes half way through) but in order to produce anything an artist needs to stick their neck out, take the risk and push through the self doubt.

What digital tablet(s) do you use?

I use a Huion model: H610PRO , which is a pad tablet for professional use. For beginners and people on a budget I recommend a pad tablet, the kind where you draw on a touch-sensitive pad with a stylus, while looking at your monitor. They can be very good and fairly cheap, especially if you get it second hand from ebay or something.

What traditional materials do you use to draw?

Sketch book and pen or pencil with White eraser. I also use oil paint, watercolor and or acrylic paint as well.
